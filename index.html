Halal-First Investment Principles

Every investment strictly avoids riba (interest), gharar (excessive uncertainty), and haram industries. Our cooperative follows Musharakah (profit-sharing) and Murabaha structures, reviewed by our retained Islamic finance specialist, CPA, and licensed attorney before any commitment is made. Non-Muslim members are equally welcome — these principles simply ensure ethical, transparent, and fair investing for all.
